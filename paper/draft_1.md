AI agents are being developed to generate scientific hypotheses, a step towards AI science that can potentially revolutionize research and scientific discovery. Regardless of how a hypothesis is generated, whether by denovo ideation or data-driven discover, the critical scientific act is its evaluation. The essential requirement is that it is expressed in a form that is falsifiable, that there is a way that an observation could prove it false. Secondly, it must be logically consistent. Finally, evaluation becomes an endevour to test it - attempt to prove it false - based on experiments, either experiments already performed or new experiments designed and executed.
